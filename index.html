GATE Facility — Internal Directive

The Non-Progression Pact

A binding agreement between all crew members of this Abiotic Factor save file.

Terms

1. No signatory shall unlock, trigger, or complete any story-critical objective — main quest steps, boss encounters, unique key items, or cutscene-gated areas — unless every signatory below is present in the session.
2. Solo or partial-crew play is permitted for base-building, resource runs, farming, decorating, and general tidying, provided no new story flag is tripped in the process.
3. A signatory who plays alone may explore areas already unlocked by the full crew, but may not push past the last shared checkpoint.
4. Any breach of this pact shall be met with a penalty decided by unanimous vote of the remaining crew, up to and including permanent teasing.
5. This pact takes effect upon the first signature below and remains binding until the final story objective is cleared together, or dissolved by unanimous agreement.
